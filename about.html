Our ambition at Pizza 4P's is not to simply be “A Great Pizza Restaurant”. We have a bigger vision – Make the World Smile for Peace. Our Mission that we stand by and work on daily in order to achieve this vision, is “Delivering Wow, Sharing Happiness”.“Wow” describes pleasant surprises and the kind of experiences that make your heart dance, leaving you full of positive energy and a motivated spirit. That excitement and positive energy works as a big step toward “Happiness”. By “Sharing” that with as many people as possible, we believe that happiness will spread from one person to another and fill the world. The outcome of "Delivering Wow, Sharing Happiness" is a smile. We work to first put a smile on the person in front of us. Then, we work to spread that smile further. If we repeat those small steps and the smiles spread over the world, we believe that the whole world can be filled with more happiness.
